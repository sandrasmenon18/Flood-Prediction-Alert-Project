Precautions

  • While facing a flood situation, avoid wading through the flowing water, unless it is really necessary.
  • If you are forced to cross a flooded place, find a long stick to check the ground surface as you walk. This would help you to avoid falling in potholes.
  • Unclog the drains, sewers, drainpipes with the help of a plunger or drain-cleaner to keep them clean.
  • Construct barricades to stop the rain water from entering your house.
  • Keep the valuable items and important documents at safe heights.
